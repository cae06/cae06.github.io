Why go searching for these shipwrecks?

Why not visit a shipwreck? Each one tells a powerful story — from its first voyage to its final, mysterious moments. Whether lying beneath the waves or stranded on land, these historic relics offer a thrilling glimpse into the past that captures the imagination and curiosity of all who explore them

What Do I Need To Know To Go To These Sunken Ships

Before visiting sunken ships, it is important to know how to get to it. Lots of sunken ships require scuba diving certification, while others can be explored by snorkeling or boat tours. You will also want proper gear, a guide if you're new, and a sense of adventure! there are often shipwrecks you can access by walking around a coast, however lots of these are slowly decaying as they age and are therefore dangerous. a rule of thumb is to always check local regulations, as some wrecks are protected sites.
