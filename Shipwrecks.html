Explore the Fishing Vessel of BEAU (located in Micronesia)

  • Wreck Type: Fishing Vessel
  • Location: Truk Lagoon (Chuuk Lagoon), Micronesia
  • Depth: Roughly 18 Metres or 59 Feet
  • Accessibility: Easily accessed for intermediate divers
  • Visibility: Generally good however not recommended to visit during bad weather conditions
  • Use?: A Fishing boat turned into Training Vessel
  • How old is it?: It sunk in February 1944
  • How Did it sink?: Sunk in a storm

The MV Beau is a lesser-known gem among Truk Lagoon's legendary wrecks. Unlike the Japanese WWII ships that dominate the area’s diving history, the Beau stands out as a modern, civilian vessel—likely sunk in the decades following the war. Despite its non-military background, the ship is upright position and preserved structure make it a fantastic and accessible dive site for all levels. This wreck is often used by local dive operators as a training site due to its safe depth, clear layout, and minimal current. It's also a refreshing change of pace for experienced divers seeking something different from the usual WWII wrecks.
